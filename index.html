Atelier
/adlˈyā/
noun

a workshop or studio, especially one used by an artist or designer.

The Atelier is the name of the team and studio that is designing Shoot,
a platform for Photographers, Videographers and Creatives
to connect and create with anyone that has a story to tell.

From beauty portraits to high fashion editorials, corporate headshots to weddings,
we strive to revolutionize how people use photography and video to tell their stories.

Because the internet and digital photography are rapidly changing the ways we communicate with and understand each others experiences,
technology and design should empower people to pursue their crafts and push art to its limits.
